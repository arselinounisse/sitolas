Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filledvwith your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Aproximadamente 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, meats, vegetables, cooked, herbs, diced

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: The Eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add tabblespoon of water or milk for a flufffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  4. Add fillings(optional): When the eggs beign to eat set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
